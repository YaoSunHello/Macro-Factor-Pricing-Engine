# Macro-Factor-Pricing-Engine
The model builds a probabilistic framework to understand asset price movements through macroeconomic drivers instead of a deterministic prediction.

## Methodology
As for value investor, the framework of asset pricing is to explain the asset price movement based on the observable macro drivers. Understanding the foundamentals is essential for pricing certain asset classes. Qualitative researchers explain the asset price movements based on the intuitive norms. By using quantitatie researching method, the intuition and reasons will be supported by numbers. However, model is never garanteed to be fully reflecting the complexity of real market movement. The factors that driving the asset price movements are impossible to fully observed. 
This framework is to support a probabilistic understanding of a range of asset price movements. The selection of the asset class is based on the accessibility of individual investor. The framework is restricted by the data availablitiy and accuracy, the market effeciency assumption, the assumptions that all asset returns follow a statistic distribution.
